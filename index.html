Career Tips & Advice

Welcome! If you're feeling stuck, unsure, or ready for something new in your life or career, you're in the right place. I’m Janine Conway, a friendly and approachable life and career coach based in Ramsbottom, offering personalised support across Bury, Bolton, and the Greater Manchester area, as well as online. I can help you move forward—wherever you’re starting from.

With over 20 years’ experience as an employability specialist, mentor, and life coach, I’ve supported a wide range of people—from young people just starting out, to adults navigating career change, to those managing health conditions or long-term unemployment. I guide you with empathy, honesty, and a deep understanding of the challenges that come with career transitions.

💼 What I Can Help With

  • Lifestyle Changes – Guidance for making improvements and implementing changes in any area of your life.
  • Career Focus & Direction – Explore your values, strengths, and goals; find clarity about what’s next; navigate career change with confidence.
  • CV & Application Support – Create or update your CV; write compelling cover letters and applications; stand out for the right reasons.
  • Interview Preparation – Build confidence and reduce nerves; practise common and tricky interview questions; present your best self.
  • Job Search Strategy – Learn where and how to look for jobs; stay organised and motivated; tap into the “hidden job market.”
  • Confidence & Mindset Coaching – Overcome self-doubt; build resilience; learn how to back yourself professionally.
  • Ongoing Accountability – Stay on track with regular sessions; get encouragement when things get tough; celebrate your wins, big and small.
