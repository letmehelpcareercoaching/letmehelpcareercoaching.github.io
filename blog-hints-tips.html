Top Tips to Get Your Name Noticed by Employers

Getting your name known by a potential employer at least three times during your job application process can really boost your chances. Here’s how:

  • When you find the job you want, apply directly on the employer’s website.
  • Look for a question to ask about the role or company, then contact the recruitment person at the company to ask that question.
  • When you make contact, be sure to give your name at that point.
  • Ask for an email address if it’s appropriate so you can send your CV and cover letter directly.
  • Send your application, and then check if it has been received.
  • Make a follow-up call after the application deadline (usually 3 days after) to enquire about the status of your application.
  • Throughout the process, be really positive and smile on the phone – they can hear it even though they can’t see you.
  • Remember the name of each person you speak to, and make your own notes to keep track.
