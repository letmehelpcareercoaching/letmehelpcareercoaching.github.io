Top Tips to Get Your Name Noticed by Employers

Getting your name known by a potential employer at least three times during your job application process can really boost your chances. Here’s how:

  1. Find the job you want and apply directly on the employer’s website.
  2. Look for a question to ask about the role or company, then contact the recruitment person at the company to ask that question.
  3. Give your name when you make contact.
  4. Ask for an email address if it’s appropriate so you can send your CV and cover letter directly.
  5. Send your application, then check if it has been received.
  6. Make a follow-up call after the application deadline (usually 3 days after) to enquire about the status of your application.
  7. Be really positive and smile on the phone – they can hear it even though they can’t see you.
  8. Remember the name of each person you speak to, and make your own notes to keep track.
